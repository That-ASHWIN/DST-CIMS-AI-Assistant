ABOUT DST-CIMS
==============

The DST-Centre for Interdisciplinary Mathematical Sciences (DST-CIMS) is an academic and research centre at Banaras Hindu University dedicated to interdisciplinary research and education in Mathematical Sciences, Statistics, Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Scientific Computing, Mathematical Modelling and related fields.

The centre promotes innovation, collaboration, academic excellence and advanced research through interdisciplinary approaches that connect mathematics, statistics, computer science and emerging technologies.

DST-CIMS aims to serve students, researchers, faculty members and academic communities through research activities, academic programmes, workshops, seminars, conferences and collaborative projects.
